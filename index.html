SOME MACHINE LEARNING USE CASES WE SOLVED

AI powered Solar Farm  Effective large scale solar power generation requires understanding of weather conditions, cloud movements among other factors for optimum power generation. The machine learning system forecasts weather and cloud movements to produces an optimized tilt angle plan for solar panels in each sectional grid so that they are moved automatically to enable better solar harvesting.

Smart Call Center line routing Call Centers account to inefficiency due to direct line routing or manual routing. Routing software powered by our deep learning model forecasts the possible duration of ongoing call and plans next call to be routed effectively. The system looks into customer history, problem type and also specific weight words used during conversation.

Intelligent Freight Management  Freight management is a complex industry with nested dependencies. The intelligent freight management system helps accessing the risks beforehand based on different factors and enables mitigation. The system also governs freight clearance lanes for optimal operation by assisting lane managers with smart orchestration.

Drug Testing without Living Hosts It is inhuman and expensive to evaluate drug combinations on living hosts. This AI system mimics protein synthesis and enables visualizing change in protein compounds when the drug is administered. It enables validating various combinations without involving living hosts.
